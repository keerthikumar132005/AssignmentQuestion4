NON-VEG FOODS INGREDIENTS
Butter Chicken Tandoor-cooked chicken in a rich tomato and buttery sauce
Chicken Tikka Marinated and grilled chicken skewers
Biryani Fragrant rice dish with spices and marinated meat (chicken, lamb, or goat)
Tandoori Chicken Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices and roasted in a tandoor oven
Fish Curry Fish cooked in a spicy and tangy gravy, often with coconut or tamarind
Mutton Korma A rich and creamy meat curry with nuts and aromatic spices
Chicken Chettinad A spicy South Indian chicken curry with a blend of spices
Chicken Shawarma Spiced and grilled chicken served in flatbreads
